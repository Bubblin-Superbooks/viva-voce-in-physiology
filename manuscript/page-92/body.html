Ans. 1gm/dL haemoglobin, when fully saturated, combines with 1.34 ml of oxygen, therefore haemoglobin concentration is an index of oxygen carrying capacity of blood. Normal values:- Males- 21ml/dL Females- 18ml/dL Adult Haemoglobin [HbA] Fetal Haemoglobin [HbF] 1.Contains 4 polypeptide chains [2 alpha and 2 beta].
