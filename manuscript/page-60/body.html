Que.4. What information can you get from ECG?

Ans 4.ECG provides useful information about the following Heart rate, rhythm and conduction of cardiac muscle Position of heart in the chest, whether horizontal, oblique or vertical. The relative size of cardiac chambers and cardiac muscle e.g. left ventricular hypertrophy Type of conduction block e.g. Left bundle or right bundle branch block.
